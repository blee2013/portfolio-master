About Me

Brittany Lee wakes up to her daily alarm of a small puppy jumping on her face. King T'Challa, her miniature pinscher, is a huge joy and keeps her moving each day often like she has had multiple triple lattes. Brittany thrives on using design work, digital organizing and strategic planning to help organizations bring their vision to the world. She has worked in reentry, Christian ministry, and the restaurant industry. She currently is helping people navigate the overwhelming need of utilizing the virtual world as it continues to exponentially grow at fast speed. Most of her free time is spent with King T'Challa, reading and getting outside. She strongly believes in helping others through empowerment and helping others thrive after trauma. Brittany lives by the motto, "Work Hard, Pray Hard!"
